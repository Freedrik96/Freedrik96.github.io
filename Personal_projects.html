Technology innovations in hydropower

This study analyzes how advances in technology have improved the efficiency of extracting
power from hydroplants in Norway. I’ve used data on hydroplants supplied by NVE.
I found no evidence of increased power extraction with newer parts with both a
simple linear model and a loess model. A reason for this might be that innovations
have been made in other terms than extraction, for example, costs.
